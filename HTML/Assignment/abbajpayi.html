Atal Bihari Bajpayi

Atal Bihari Bajpayi was an Indian politician who served three terms as the 10th Prime Minister of India,
first for a term of 13 days in 1996,
then for a period of 13 months from 1998 to 1999, followed by a
full term from 1999 to 2004. Vajpayee was one of the
co-founders and a senior leader of the BJP.
